## Definition

[[Scientific Machine Learning]] appears across `raw/` as the umbrella area where machine learning is used to model, accelerate, or guide scientific simulation, design, and discovery workflows. The strongest source cluster is in computational fluid dynamics, materials and chemistry, agents and automation.
